You are Earth’s last chance for survival.

Prism Shell is a high intensity sci-fi action game with intuitive touch controls. Hold your finger on the screen to fire at the alien hordes, and tap glowing points to glide across the battlefield in your high-powered hover tank. If you’re really in trouble, tap your tank to enter Hyper Mode, where you can fire a massive, powerful laser. Dodge alien attacks, shift into better firing positions, and collect currency for upgrades. Can you and Major Beretta pilot the Prism Shell to victory?

Features:

  • You’ll be fighting the aliens in no time with easy-to-learn touch controls
  • Earn in-game currency to power up your tank – no in-app purchases required!
  • Take control of the alien-destroying laser to turn the tables on your foes
  • Multiple aliens to fight with different behavior types and attack methods
